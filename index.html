A Glimpse of A Simple Story That Led to Forever

Our First Meeting

Our first meeting was simple.
I asked for a chair at the restaurant, my back hurt, and I needed to sit.
Just a small moment,
But that's how it all began.
A quiet beginning to a story I never saw coming.
- Bow

It all started with a simple, ordinary dinner on a pretty normal night. A conversation that felt iconic in its own way. She asked for a chair to sit, and without even realizing it, I gave her my life

First Official Day

I looked at him and asked,
"What are we right now?"
Then he asked me to be his girlfriend!!!
- Bow

Since the day we first met, you could count on one hand the days we didn't see each other. Time flew by, and little by little, a mutual understanding quietly started to grow. Then Nov 5, 2022, she said yes for the first time — a simple word that changed everything.
- Leo

The Proposal Day

I thought we were just going on a trip for my birthday.
But along the way, he was acting so strangely.
That's when I knew. Something special was about to happen.
- Bow

"It was probably the most nervous and excited I've ever felt. I planned everything by the beach, hoping everything would be perfect. And when the fireworks burst into the sky, with a YES. A moment I'll never forget, made even more special because the very next day was her birthday."
- Leo
